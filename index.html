About Me

My name is Diyuan Wu (吴迪远 in Chinese), I'm currently a PhD student in Institute of Science and Technology Austria (ISTA) starting from fall, 2022 . I'm very fortunate to work with Professor Marco Mondelli . I'm research interest is theory of (deep) machine learning.

Prior to my PhD journey in ISTA, I received a master degree in communication system from EPFL in 2022, and bachelor degree in information and communication engineering from UESTC(电子科大), Chengdu, China in 2019. I also did a scientific internship in ISTA to finish my master's thesis from 2021-2022, also with Professor Marco Mondelli.
