Introduction

My name is Diyuan Wu (吴迪远 in Chinese), I'm currently a PhD student in Institute of Science and Technology Austria (ISTA). I'm very fortunate to be supervised by Professor Marco Mondelli. I'm boardly interested in theory of (deep) machine learning, especially understanding dynamical properties in learning problems. Currently my focus are on:

  • Data privacy in training neural networks, especially in mean-field regime

  • Understanding training dynamics for transformer models

Prior to my PhD journey in ISTA, I received a master degree in communication system from École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne(EPFL) in 2022, and bachelor degree in information and communication engineering from UESTC(电子科大) in 2019.
